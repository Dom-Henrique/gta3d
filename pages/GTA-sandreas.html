Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas

About the Game

Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is an action-adventure game released in 2004. It was developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. The game takes place in San Andreas, a fictional state inspired by California and Nevada during the early 1990s.

Main Character

The main character is Carl "CJ" Johnson, a young man who returns to his hometown after his mother's murder and gets pulled back into gang life and street crime.

Gameplay

  • Open-world exploration across three cities
  • Story missions
  • Driving cars, motorcycles, planes, and boats
  • Weapon combat
  • Side activities and businesses
  • RPG elements like fitness, stamina, and skills

Interesting Fact

San Andreas is famous for its massive open world, deep customization options, and being one of the best-selling and most influential games in the GTA series.
