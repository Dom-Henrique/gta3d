Grand Theft Auto: Vice City

About the Game

Grand Theft Auto: Vice City is an action-adventure game released in 2002. It was developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. The game takes place in Vice City, a fictional city inspired by Miami during the 1980s.

Main Character

The main character is Tommy Vercetti, a former criminal who works to build his own criminal empire after being released from prison.

Gameplay

  • Open-world exploration
  • Story missions
  • Driving cars, motorcycles, and boats
  • Weapon combat
  • Side activities and businesses

Interesting Fact

Vice City is famous for its colorful 1980s atmosphere, memorable soundtrack, and one of the most iconic stories in the GTA series.
